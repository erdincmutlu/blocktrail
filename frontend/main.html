About Us

Children make up a third of the world's population but half of all refugees.
Women and children are concerned that their healthcare records might be shared with the Home Office without their consent.
This leads to lack of confidence and treatment.

Our idea

1. Trail of ID documents stored as smart contracts
2. Control over sharing of Information
3. Access to communities and healthcare services

100000 women & children arrived in the UK in the last 5 years. BlockTrail gives them access to a community and helps them to create a new identity.

Start building identity portfolio in their host country, retain control over the sharing of information and find information on services available on arrival.
